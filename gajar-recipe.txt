Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds carrot
 - 200 ml milk
 - 500 gm sugar
 - 200 ml Ghee

 Directions:
 1. Shred the carrot in small pieces using shredder.
 2. Cook the shredded carrot in a kadai and add ghee to it.
 3. Once it is properly cooked add milk and sugar to it .
 4. cool until carrot becomes dark red .
